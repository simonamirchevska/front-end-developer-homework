Thesis: Dispersed Nature Museum

New museology is a theoretical and philosophical movement associated with the change of focus from the previous idea of a museum as a institution, to a new discourse about its social role, which incite new ways of communication and new styles of expression, different from the classical museum models of placing the museum collection inside of a building. This kind of approach re-evalues the museum’s contents, is setting a different social aspect and it is integrating it with social groups and finally, it is extending the idea of a single physical limited space with the meaning of the intangible heritage and the subjectivity of the observer.
          - André Desvallées and François Mairesse, ed. Key Concepts of Museology. Icom, 2009.
